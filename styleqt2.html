Lionel Andres Messi is a professional football player who played as a forward for the Argentina national team and Spanish club FC Barcelona and will now play for Paris Saint-Germain. Born on 24 June 1987 in Rosario, Argentina, Messi is considered as one of the best footballers of this generation and greatest of all time. Messi has five FIFA Ballon dOr to his name, matched only by his rival Cristiano Ronaldo. A versatile attacker, Messi plays on the right-wing or as a center-forward. Nicknamed La Pulga, he has scored 672 senior career goals from 778 appearances to date.
